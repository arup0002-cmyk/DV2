Smartphone & Internet Usage in Australia

Exploring how age, location, and lifestyle shape digital connectivity.

The Rise of Connectivity

Internet access has grown dramatically across Australia over the last few decades, becoming an essential utility rather than a luxury. This trend sets the stage for how smartphones dominate daily life.

Location: The Digital Divide

Where you live in Australia dictates how you use your devices. Urban hubs show vastly different usage patterns compared to regional or remote areas.

Regional Access: Notice how coastal and heavily urbanised states exhibit different connectivity levels compared to remote territories.
Activity Density: Western Australia and Victoria show intense engagement across gaming and apps compared to other regions.

Age & Lifestyle: What are we doing online?

A smartphone is no longer just for calling. The reasons Australians access the internet shift drastically as they age.

Lifestyle Shifts: The explosive growth in health services and entertainment usage between 2014 and 2016 highlights our changing priorities.
Growing Up Digital: As children transition into their teenage years, app usage and texting rapidly overtake basic gaming.
